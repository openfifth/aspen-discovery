### Koha Updates
#### Checkout renewal and hold fee display
- Display the renewal fee (as set in Koha) both before checkout renewal.  ([DIS-2349](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-2349)) (*CZ*)
- Ensure the renewal fee displayed accounts for the discount that applies per Koha's sys prefs and circulation rules.  ([DIS-2349](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-2349)) (*CZ*)
- Display the hold fee (as set in Koha) both before and after a hold is placed.  ([DIS-1345](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1345)) (*CZ*)
- Ensure the hold fee accounts for the applicable Koha TitleHoldFeeStrategy.  ([DIS-1345](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1345)) (*CZ*)
#### Patron Account Renewal (ILS-Based)
##### Configuration
- An "ILS Based Card Renewal" option is now available in Card Renewal settings, offered only when the connected ILS supports an in-Aspen renewal flow (currently Koha). ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- The "Redirect to Card Renewal URL" option is hidden for ILSs that do not support card renewal. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
##### Patron Self-Renewal Flow
- Expired patrons continue to see the "Expired" warning on their account dashboard, and a renewal button beneath it now starts the in-Aspen renewal process at `/MyAccount/AccountRenewal`. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- The renewal page presents a guided stepper. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
    - a "Start" step introduces the process.
    - a "Verification Questions" step is shown only when a self-renewal information message is configured in the ILS; the patron must confirm before continuing, and is shown a warning if they decline.
    - a "Confirm Contact Information" step reuses the existing Contact Information form, pre-populated from the ILS, so the patron can review and optionally update their details before submitting.
    - a "Request Submitted" step confirms the outcome and displays the new expiration date returned by the ILS.
- Submitting the request renews the account through the ILS. When contact fields are changed, the updates are sent to the ILS and handled per its configuration (review queue or direct update). ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- On successful renewal the patron's expiry state is refreshed immediately, so the "Expired" warning is gone on return to the MyAccount home page. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- If the ILS rejects the submission, the patron stays on the Confirm Contact Information step and the specific error returned by the ILS is displayed. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- Back and Cancel navigation is supported throughout. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)
- All messages, headers, and buttons added by the renewal flow are available for translation. ([DIS-1938](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1938)) (*CZ*)


### Usage Updates
#### Usage Graph Reporting Periods
- The General Aspen Usage, API Usage, Sideloads Usage, Summon Usage, Materials Request Usage, and Axis360 Usage graphs can now be displayed and downloaded grouped by a selectable reporting period. ([DIS-1689](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1689)) (*CZ*)
    - "Month" remains available and is the default, preserving the existing behaviour.
    - "Day" and "Year" group the same metrics by the respective period.
    - "Custom" groups usage into fixed-length periods defined by a start date and a duration in days.
- The usage data underlying these graphs now records the day, in addition to the month and year, so day-level reporting is possible. ([DIS-1689](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1689)) (*CZ*)
    - usage recorded before this change is attributed to day 0 of its month so that historical data remains viewable under the new periods.
- Dates shown on the usage graphs and in their CSV downloads are formatted according to the configured locale. ([DIS-1689](https://aspen-discovery.atlassian.net/browse/DIS-1689)) (*CZ*)
